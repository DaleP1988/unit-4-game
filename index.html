Crystals Collector!

How to Play the Game


♦ You will be given a number at the start of the game. There are four Crystals Collector cards below. Select and click a crystal name to get points in your total score.

♦ If you can match your total score to the given number, you win! If your total score passes the given number, you lose.

♦ When you start a new game, each crystal card is allotted specific, new values.
